This is a simple application made with react 18 that shows a list of heroes and villains from various comic book publishers obtained through the "superhero-api", focusing mainly on Marvel and DC. It has a fake login to practice with private and public routes.

![Comics Database](/images/comics.webp)

[View Project>](https://react18-heroes.vercel.app/)
[View Source Code>](https://github.com/jesus-molano/react18-heroes)

- [Vite 3](https://vitejs.dev/)
- [Bootstrap 5](https://getbootstrap.com/)
- [React Router 6](https://reactrouter.com/)
- [Chart.js-2](https://www.chartjs.org/)
